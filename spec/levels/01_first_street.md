## Visual Identity

A simple open-air parking lot with grass borders. This is the starter level, intentionally simple to teach basic mechanics. The layout features:
- Asphalt parking surface
- Grass perimeter
- Yellow road markings at entry/exit

## Challenges

### Bronze: First Thousand
Earn $1,000 profit in a day - Revenue target

### Silver: Steady Service
Complete 25 charging sessions - Counter target

### Gold: Quality Operator
Earn $5,000 while maintaining 80%+ rating - Combo challenge

## Strategy Tips

- Start with L2 chargers - they're affordable and have good throughput
- The 1500 kVA limit is very generous - power management is not a concern here
- Focus on learning the basics: placing chargers, managing customers, maintaining equipment
- This is a good site to experiment with different layouts before moving to harder sites
