## Visual Identity

A converted gas station with distinctive visual elements:
- Large red canopy covering the main charging area
- Convenience store building with blue accents
- Disabled gas pumps (converted to EV charging)
- Gas station sign, dumpster area, decorative trees
- Worn asphalt with skid marks showing heavy use

## Challenges

### Bronze: Quick Turnover
Complete 40 charging sessions - High-traffic location means lots of customers

### Silver: Profit Center
Earn $5,000 in revenue - DCFC chargers bring in premium rates

### Gold: Happy Customers
Complete 50 sessions with no angry departures - Quality service focus

## Strategy Tips

- High traffic (85 popularity) means consistent demand
- Mix of DCFC and L2 chargers serves different customer needs
- The canopy area is perfect for premium fast chargers
- Watch the 500 kVA limit - you can't go crazy with multiple 350kW units
- The convenience store can generate additional foot traffic
