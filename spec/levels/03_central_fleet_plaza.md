## Visual Identity

A massive commercial fleet charging facility:
- Largest site in the game (30x20)
- Industrial concrete yard
- Loading zones for fleet vehicles
- Dumpster area for waste management
- Extensive industrial lighting
- Warehouse/depot aesthetic

## Challenges

### Bronze: Fleet Manager
Complete 100 charging sessions - Massive volume target

### Silver: Industrial Scale
Earn $25,000 in revenue - Large-scale operation

### Gold: Fleet Commander
Earn $50,000 at 85%+ rating - The ultimate revenue challenge

## Strategy Tips

- This is the ULTIMATE CHALLENGE - largest site, highest stakes
- 3 MW (3000 kVA) connection is the most power in the game
- Shift-change demand spikes require careful power management
- Multiple high-power chargers are essential for fleet operations
- The 100-session Bronze challenge shows the scale of operation
- $50,000 Gold target is the highest in the game
- Battery storage is almost mandatory to handle shift-change peaks
